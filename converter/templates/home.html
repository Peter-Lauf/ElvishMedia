About our Project

Elvishmedia... Just why?!

Because people got tired of their media files playing hard-to-get in the format they need! But they shall fear not, our media converting site is here to rescue them from the clutches of format incompatibility!
Click the "more info button" for... well for more info.

Once upon a time, in a far-off land of magic and wonder, there were four IT students named Peter, Rihhard, Siim, and Chris. They were tasked with creating a group project. They pondered and pondered what it may be, when Crhis drew his sword, pointed it to the sky and cried "MEDIAAA CONVERTEEEER". They then all knew what they must do. Create a website that could convert media files from one format to another. They knew that this was no ordinary task, as they would need to use their magical abilities to create a website that could convert even the most complex of media files. Rihhard was a powerful wizard, who could manipulate code with his spells. Chris was a cunning warlock, who could use his dark magic to create powerful algorithms. Peter was a wise sorcerer, who could tap into the knowledge of the ancient ones to create new front-end designs. Siim was a brave knight, who could defend the website from the attacks. Together, they began their work. They created a magical portal that could access the media files from any corner of the land. They then used their powers to create a powerful converting logic that could turn any media file into any format*.

But their work was not without its challenges. As they worked, dark creatures known as the "Pro Crestinateurs" began to attack their website, trying to corrupt their codes and destroy their work. For evil cannot create, it can only corrupt. The four IT students fought bravely against the Pro Crestinateurs, using their magical powers to create their website. They battled for days, and even when it seemed as if all was lost, they never gave up.

Finally, after a long and grueling battle, the Pro Crestinateurs were defeated. Their website was safe once again, and they had successfully created the most powerful media converting website in all the land.

Word of their incredible work spread throughout the kingdom, and soon people from all corners of the land came to use their website. It became so popular that even the rulers of the kingdom of Goggol came to ask for their help. And so, the four IT students became known as the "Magical Media Wizards". They had not only completed their task, but had become heroes in their own right, using their magical abilities to help the people of the land.
